Core Team

The Core Team is the permanent foundation of the department, comprised of directors, project managers, designers, and developers. Directors lead across operations, business, design, and technology, while senior-level staff manage multiple projects—guiding and collaborating with Flex and Specialist team members to deliver cohesive, high-quality work.

Flex Team

The Flex Team is comprised of cross-departmental colleagues and trusted external collaborators: creative technologists, software developers, AV integrators, fabricators, lighting and sound specialists, and others. This team scales with each project's demands, drawing on proven collaborators to bring the right expertise to each engagement.

Specialist Team

The Specialist Team is engaged on a per-project basis, brought in because their expertise fits the needs of a particular project or client. Artists, content curators, technical subject matter experts, client vendors, and other specialists fall into this category — pinch hitters whose involvement is purposeful and project-specific rather than ongoing.

Software Tools

A library of proven software solutions covering content management, control systems, content distribution, messaging, sensor processing, and monitoring. These tools are built and refined across projects, reducing setup time and providing an adaptable foundation for new engagements.

Hardware Tools

A standardized inventory of display and interactive hardware including digital signage players, high-end graphics endpoints, LCD/LED panels, video walls, LED walls, projectors, and sensors. Maintaining a curated hardware ecosystem streamlines procurement, integration, and long-term support across projects.

Architectural Element Designs

Documented and reusable physical form factor designs for kiosks, screen housings, touch tables, photo booths, and custom controllers. These templates accelerate fabrication and design decisions while ensuring consistent quality and adaptability across environments.

Design Elements

A growing library of visual and interactive building blocks: generative effects, text effects, scene transitions, post-processing, and a shared design language of fonts, color palettes, and icons. Drawing from this library speeds up production and maintains a cohesive aesthetic that can be tailored to each project.

Parallel Build / Project Funded

Reusable components are developed in parallel with active projects, funded by the work that first requires them. This allows the department to grow its library organically with minimal R&D overhead, with each project contributing back to a shared foundation.

From small-scale lab mockups and partner prototypes to full-scale builds and working VR environments, these tools allow the team to vet concepts, determine feasibility, and iterate under conditions close to real deployment — while also serving as a showroom to bring clients into the vision and make the sale.

The portfolio is organized as a tiered system, where each level supports the one above it. At the top, signature projects define the brand, earn recognition, and drive new business. The middle tier of standard projects builds on reusable modules to deliver efficient, high-quality work at scale. At the bottom, turnkey projects — quickly configured and themed for any context — generate healthy margins and create a strong foundation.
